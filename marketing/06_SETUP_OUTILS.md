# RIFT — Setup & Outils Gratuits

## 🛠️ OUTILS À INSTALLER

### Video Editing
1. **CapCut** (Free, recommended)
   - Download: https://www.capcut.com/
   - Why: Fast, intuitive, exports en tous formats
   - Setup: 15 minutes pour prendre en main
   - Template disponibles pour shorts

2. **DaVinci Resolve** (Free, pro-level)
   - Download: https://www.davinciresolve.com/ (version gratuite)
   - Why: Plus powerful que CapCut si tu veux qualité cinéma
   - Setup: 30 minutes, interface un peu compliquée
   - Good for: Trailer complet, color grading

### Graphics & Templates
3. **Canva** (Free version)
   - Website: https://www.canva.com/
   - Why: Drag-and-drop, templates prêtes
   - Setup: Créer compte, chercher "social media" templates
   - Limitation: Resolution/quality less than Pro, but good enough
   - Recommendation: Start free, passer à Pro ($13/mois) après 1 mois

### Video Recording
4. **Screen Recorder Builtin** (Windows)
   - Windows Key + G (Xbox Game Bar)
   - Or: Settings → System → Sound → Record Audio
   - Simple pour enregistrer gameplay directement

5. **OBS Studio** (Free, open-source)
   - Download: https://obsproject.com/
   - Why: Plus professionnel pour multi-source, streaming (optional)
   - Setup: 45 minutes (possible learning curve)
   - Optional si tu veux vraiment polished

### Audio
6. **Audacity** (Free, open-source)
   - Download: https://www.audacityteam.org/
   - Why: Edit audio, remove background noise, mix tracks
   - Setup: 20 minutes
   - Optional mais utile si voiceover ou sound design

### Social Scheduling
7. **Buffer** (Free plan)
   - Website: https://buffer.com/
   - Why: Schedule posts across all platforms at once
   - Setup: 10 minutes
   - Limit: Free plan = 3 posts per day, 3 social profiles max

8. **Meta Business Suite** (Free, built-in)
   - Website: https://business.instagram.com/
   - Why: Native scheduling for Instagram/Facebook
   - Setup: 10 minutes (link your Instagram)
   - Good for: If you're doing Instagram + Facebook only

### Analytics & Tracking
9. **Google Sheets** (Free)
   - Website: https://sheets.google.com/
   - Why: Track KPIs manually, see trends
   - Setup: 5 minutes
   - What to track: Views, CTR, installs, retention

10. **Bitly** (Free)
    - Website: https://bitly.com/
    - Why: Shorten URLs + track clicks
    - Setup: 5 minutes
    - How to use: Create short links for Play Store, track engagement

11. **Google Form** (Free)
    - Website: https://forms.google.com/
    - Why: Collect feedback, survey community
    - Setup: 5 minutes per form
    - Optional: Monthly survey "What feature do you want next"

### Community Management
12. **Discord** (Free server)
    - Website: https://discord.com/
    - Why: Host your game community
    - Setup: 30 minutes (server setup + roles + channels)
    - Channels to create: announcements, gameplay, leaderboard, bugs, general

## 🎬 PRODUCTION WORKFLOW (Per Video)

### Step 1: Prep (10 min)
1. Open CapCut
2. Create new project (9:16 format for mobile)
3. Import raw footage
4. Rough-cut into scenes (drag and drop)

### Step 2: Edit (20-30 min)
1. Add music: CapCut has library, or import from YouTube Audio
2. Trim scenes to match music beat
3. Add text overlays (transitions, titles)
4. Color adjust if needed (CapCut simple filters)
5. Add sound effects (from library)

### Step 3: Export (5-10 min)
1. Export as MP4 (1080p 30fps minimum)
2. Generate 3 versions: 9:16 (TikTok), 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (Instagram)
3. Keep originals in backup folder

### Step 4: Post (5-10 min per platform)
1. Upload to TikTok with caption + hashtags
2. Share to Instagram Reels (auto-convert from TikTok or manually)
3. Add to YouTube Shorts + description
4. Schedule on Buffer for Reddit/Twitter if using

**Total time per video: 1-2 hours** (gets faster with repetition)

## 🎯 CRITICAL PATH (What Actually Matters)

**To start:**
1. Record gameplay (30 min)
2. Use CapCut to edit (1 hour per video)
3. Post with good caption (5 min)
4. Reply to comments (10 min)

That's it. Don't overthink.

**Tools that give 80% of the effort:**
- CapCut (video)
- Canva (graphics)
- Google Sheets (tracking)
- Buffer (scheduling)

These 4 tools + discipline = success.

## 💡 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Recording low-quality footage** → Record at 1080p or higher
2. **Posting without captions** → Always 100% captions (accessibility + sound-off viewing)
3. **Same hashtags every time** → Rotate, use analytics to see what works
4. **No CTA** → Always link somewhere (Play Store, Discord, etc)
5. **Ignoring comments** → Reply within 1 hour if possible (algorithm boost)
6. **Editing forever** → Good enough > perfect. Post and iterate.
7. **Not tracking metrics** → Create simple Google Sheet, update weekly
8. **Posting when tired** → Schedule in advance, post when fresh
9. **Comparing to big creators** → They have teams, you're solo. Growth is slower, that's ok.
10. **Giving up after 2 weeks** → Marketing takes 4-8 weeks to see trends
